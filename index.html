Donation

A lot of resilience, patience, and will is required to pull off the task to maintaining an indie website


Specially designed for my friends. Which requires a lot of resources and enormous amount of passion to do it


I created this website on 2015/12/28 as a dream to bring all of my school friends together


   But as this project has progressively matured some of my friends has showed interest to help me in this pursuit. They may not helped in coding the site functionalities but have provided invaluable support. Likewise, in helping out bringing the PDFs of the magazines which we had received during the school days. As a way to preserve our school life !

  Thus, I need your help to enrich the site with more functionalities and maybe even provide this website technology open to others as a way to enrich and preserve their school life, like I did. I need monetary support to keep providing fees to our CDNs(Cloud Delivery Networks) which caches the site resources in their servers to provide smooth 24x7 site uptime. And even likewise we can upgrade our domain from “.esy.es” to “.org” or “.com”. With Your donation we will be step closer to achieve the goal of providing an open source free service to let people preserve their sweet nostalgic memories
